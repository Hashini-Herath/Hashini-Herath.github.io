About

Hi, I'm Hashini Herath, a dynamic undergraduate with a passion for technology, innovation, and leadership. Currently pursuing a BSc. Serving as the Secretary of the Rotaract Club of Kandy Metropolitan for RI Year 2025-26, I collaborate with diverse teams to drive impactful initiatives.

I see every opportunity as a chance to grow, innovate, and make meaningful contributions. Whether it’s leading a team, solving complex problems, or learning a new skill, I’m always up for a challenge.
